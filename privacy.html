This Privacy Policy explains what personal information Totem collects, why, how it's used and protected, and what rights you have — in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

Who this applies to

Totem is used by clubs and schools ("the Organization") to manage sports team selection, fixtures, and results. The Organization is generally responsible for the personal information of its players, coaches, and staff entered into Totem, including obtaining any necessary consent — see "Children's information" below. Totem (operated by [Hyperianlabs — confirm exact registered entity name]) processes this information on the Organization's behalf.

What we collect

  • Players: name, date of birth, sport/position, performance ratings, VO2 max (if entered), fixture and results history.
  • Staff/coaches: name, email address, phone number (if entered).
  • Account holders: email address, and any information provided at signup (club/school name).

Totem does not collect payment card details directly — if your organization is on a paid plan, that's handled entirely by Stripe, who has their own privacy practices.

Why we process this information

  • To operate the core service — team selection, fixture scheduling, results tracking, and coach/parent-facing print sheets.
  • To send result notifications by email to relevant coaches and staff.
  • To operate free/paid plan limits and, where applicable, billing.

Children's information

Much of the information in Totem relates to children (anyone under 18). POPIA requires a valid legal basis to process a child's personal information — generally, consent from a parent or guardian, unless another exception applies. Obtaining this consent is the Organization's responsibility, since players and parents do not use Totem directly. Organizations are asked to confirm this when creating their account, and a consent form template is provided to help.

  • Resend — sends result-notification emails.
  • Stripe — payment processing, for organizations on a paid plan.
